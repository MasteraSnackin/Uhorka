After you register/log in you will be thrown directly into gameplay.

The Uhorka is a flow oriented fast pace game where You are the “Uhorka” facing difficulties of 3 levels looking to obtain secretly overpowered cannon hidden in maze which is crucial for destroying the main boss Nokia.

Although, Nokia is undestructible! It only suffer from low battery and during this phase it can’t hurt anyone so please consider it as game over.

Leaderboards are based on time of gameplay as you must focus on precision.
and speed.

Lvl 1 is pipe like structure where “Uhorka” is flying to the left side of the screen and have to avoid
90degrees walls (user have to press button and don’t hit the wall or fall into the hole behind the wall)

Lvl 2 there will be another pipe like structure where “Uhorka” is the player mouse cursor and have to
move it through without hitting the wall. If hit the wall looses one HP.

Lvl 3 will be maze where blue walls are accessible only from one direction and don’t count as crossroads
you have to use WASD or arrows to navigate through the maze (after pressing WASD or arrows you move
to the direction and stop at the next crossroad) at the end u get the cannon.

*BOSS FIGHT* you have to face Nokia and with your special cannon and drain its battery which have to
be hit at least 20 times while dashing its attacks. After fight your honor will appear in leaderboards.
